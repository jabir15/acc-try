THE COLLEGE CODE of Provincialised Colleges of ASSAM is an all comprehensive e-package containing various information collated and hosted for benefit of the employees of provincialised colleges of Assam.

Letters issued by DHE, Assam concerning the employees of provincialised colleges of Assam are made available in this e-package.

This e-package is intended to provide easily accessible information along with relevant guidelines and application formats on the following topics:

  • Revised structures of pay and allowances as per UGC Regulations,2010 and Assam Services Revision of Pay (ROP). Rules 2010
  • Acts and Rules pertinent to provincialised colleges of Assam including the Acts and Statutes of the Universities of North Eastern Region.
  • Guidelines of promotions as per CAS of the Govt. of Assam, API Score under PBAS, OPs/RCs, SSS/SWS Programmes, Seminars and Workshops under FIP.
  • Financial grants from UGC and other sources.
  • All kinds of Pensions, Gratuity, GIS and Leave Encashment.

This e-package includes files to prepare Pay Fixation Forms and to calculate revised salary including arrears. A rate reckoner is provided to know the prevalent rates of different items related to pay and allowances.

Leave Rules are explained in this e-package though questions and answers.

Hyperlinks of different "academic journals", "academic web sites" and that of the "provincialised colleges of Assam" are provided in this e-package for users' benefit.
